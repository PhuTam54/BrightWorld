Light bulbs in general have long become important items in our human life. With the continuous development of science and technology, people are increasingly upgrading the surrounding objects, including light bulbs. LED lights are the result of that process. Let's take a look at 10 famous led light brands in the world along with their journey of "conquering the light"!

Philips LED light brand

Philips is known as a large Dutch corporation, mainly operating in areas such as consumer life, health care and lighting equipment manufacturing and trading. Established in the late 19th century, the Philips brand has always stood firmly in the market with high quality lighting equipment. The peak was in 2012, this royal corporation had the top revenue in the world in the field of manufacturing lighting equipment: led lights, table lamps,... Philips truly deserves the title. "The most famous LED brand in the world".

Panasonic LED light brand

It would be remiss not to mention the famous led brand Panasonic in this list. Panasonic Corporation is the parent company of a chain of companies specializing in the production and sales of high-performance lighting equipment. It was this subsidiary that created electronic ballast control systems for CFLs as well as fluorescent lamps. These components are used in many countries around the world.

Cree LED Lights

Cree is a famous brand of distribution founded by researchers at North Carolina State University, USA. The key products of this brand are semiconductor devices for the LED lighting industry and export of high-value products to other countries. Cree once "shocked" when it released XLamp XT-E technology, capable of providing twice as much light as LED products at the same price.

Havells Sylvania

Havells Sylvania is also one of the world's leading famous led lighting companies with subsidiaries distribution across many countries in all continents: Europe, Asia, North Africa, Central and South America. Havells Sylvania was established in 1900 with a long-term effort: from a supplier of lamp replacement services to developing into a manufacturer and distributor.

OSRAM

OSRAM Multinational Corporation - OSRAM Light AG was officially established in 1906 with the main field of activity is manufacturing and trading lighting equipment. The name OSRAM is a combination of two words "Osmium" and "Wolfram" - two elements widely used in the lighting industry at that time.

Zumtobel

Zumtobel – one of the famous led light brands in the world with many years of experience in the field of lighting industry. Its products usually focus on indoor and outdoor lighting systems as well as other components. Besides, this brand of products from Austria is also involved in the research of current advanced LED and OLED technology.
